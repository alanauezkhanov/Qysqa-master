Sustainable Development and Green Economy

2 sources

These texts present the concept of sustainable development, emphasizing its link to the natural environment as the foundation of the economy. They describe the ecological-economic system and explore the idea of a green economy as a model for achieving sustainability. The sources highlight the importance of natural capital alongside other forms of capital (produced, human, and social) in driving long-term economic growth. They also discuss the potential for job creation and economic benefits through investment in various green sectors like renewable energy, sustainable transport, and green buildings.
